QUESTIONS ON YOUR MIND?



What is Six Sigma?

Six Sigma is a problem solving methodology which is centered around defect reduction and variation management; executed in the form of a disciplined, data driven and time-bound project.


What is Analytics?

Analytics is the science of using data to build models that lead to better decisions that add value to individuals, to companies, to institutions.
