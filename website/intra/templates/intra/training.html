PUBLIC TRAININGS - CLASSROOM MODEL


Foundation Level

Ideal for individuals who love to question the status quo, and in effort, want to be more methodical in their approach. People at this level serve as team members and lead small scale projects within the organization.


Six Sigma Green Belt

For more than three decades Six Sigma has played a vital role in the central business strategies of leading global players across industries. This is your first step towards getting started on an exciting journey which is evergreen and always seeking the right talent.
Our Green Belt program covers 36 hours of classroom training and total of 20 hours dedicated towards pre-course work and project work.

Analytics Starter

Availability of large amount of data around the smallest of activities has created opportunities that never existed earlier. Now the companies are not just competing on revenue, they are competing on their ability to gather data, and to draw meaningful insights from it. Therefore, they hire experts who can help in this endeavor.
Total program duration is 60 hours which covers 32 hours of classroom training + 28 hours of reinforcement through mentor led sessions and self learning.


Advanced Level

Building further on the strong foundation, this level brings newer depth and dimensions of expertise in the area of Six Sigma and Analytics. Here you learn to lead projects all by yourself, you become a change agent for your organization.


Six Sigma Black Belt

Black Belts are pretty much self-sufficient and motivated individuals who have a strong bent towards driving breakthrough improvements. These individuals lead from the front and carry a chain reaction throughout the organization.
Our Black Belt program covers 60 hours of classroom contact and another 80 hours allocated to pre, break and post session practice through video lectures, exercises and project work. The 60 hours of classroom contact is divided into two parts over a period of two months.

Analytics Pro

At this level you learn to handle large amount of data with ease. You learn advanced machine learning and predictive modeling techniques to be able to come up with right recommendations for your organization in order to save millions of dollars.
This program covers 108 hours of classroom contact over a period of six months + 32 hours reinforcement through mentor led sessions and self learning. Between the contact sessions there are practice exercises and assignments.
